The Untold Story of a Legend

From the only girl in the skatepark to the first woman on Thrasher's cover. From Olympic snowboarder to X Games gold medalist. This is the remarkable journey of Cara-Beth Burnside - a pioneer who didn't just break barriers, she obliterated them.

Written by Lotus Burnquist, daughter of skateboarding legend Bob Burnquist, this intimate biography captures CB's extraordinary life through exclusive photos and untold stories from the woman who changed action sports forever.

Exclusive Preview

"In skateboarding history, where raw talent meets unrelenting determination, few names shine as brilliantly as Cara-Beth Burnside's. Yet, her influence extends far beyond the ramps and skateparks, reaching heights that many are still unaware of.

As a young girl deeply passionate about skateboarding, she faced the weight of societal expectations pressing heavily upon her. Growing up in a world filled with stereotypes and gender biases, she confronted an uphill battle. Skateparks, predominantly male-dominated spaces, offered little sanctuary. Yet, CB possessed an unwavering spirit, fueled by her unyielding belief in her own potential."
