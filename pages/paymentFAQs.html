Payment & Pricing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe, a leading payment processor used by millions of businesses worldwide. You can pay with any major credit or debit card.

For monthly programs, your card is charged automatically each month. For package programs (3 or 6 months), the full amount is charged upfront.

Package programs offer significant savings as a reward for your commitment:

  • 3-Month Package: Save $900 (15% discount)
  • 6-Month Package: Save $3,000 (25% discount)

Longer commitments also tend to produce better results, as they give us more time to work on comprehensive skill development and career strategy.

We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee on all programs. If you're not completely satisfied within the first 14 days, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.

After 14 days, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis for exceptional circumstances.

Application & Approval

Elite mentorship is limited to just 5 students per quarter to ensure maximum attention and results. The application process helps ensure:

  • You're ready for intensive training
  • Your goals align with what we offer
  • You'll get maximum value from the program

CB personally reviews every Elite application within 48 hours. You'll receive an email with the decision and next steps.

Community program applications are typically approved within 24 hours.

If you're not quite ready for Elite coaching, we'll provide specific feedback on areas to develop. We often recommend:

  • Starting with our Accelerator program
  • Working on specific skills for 3-6 months
  • Reapplying with updated videos

Many successful Elite students started in community programs first!

Program Details

You'll need:

  • A smartphone or camera to record skating videos
  • Reliable internet for video calls
  • A device with camera/microphone for coaching calls
  • Your skateboard and safety gear

That's it! We'll guide you on the best angles and techniques for video submissions.

You'll submit videos of your skating through our platform. CB uses professional video analysis software to:

  • Break down your technique frame-by-frame
  • Add visual annotations and corrections
  • Compare your progress over time
  • Provide specific drills for improvement

Elite students can submit unlimited videos. Other programs have monthly limits.

Package programs cannot be paused but include built-in flexibility for missed sessions.
