Why Train with CB?

I'm not just another coach. I'm Cara-Beth Burnside, and I've spent decades breaking barriers in skateboarding. As the first woman to grace the cover of Thrasher Magazine and a Hall of Fame inductee, I've mastered not just the tricks, but the mindset, business acumen, and resilience needed to thrive as a professional athlete.

My coaching isn't for everyone. It's for the dedicated few who are ready to transform their raw talent into a professional career. If you're serious about becoming not just a great skater, but a game-changing athlete, you're in the right place.
